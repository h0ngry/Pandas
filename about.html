About Endangered Animals:

An endangered species is defined as a species that is at risk of extinction due ot a number of contributing causes. These causes include, but are not limited to, human activity, changes in climate, changes in food chain balance, and destruction of habitat. These species are likely to die out if conservation efforts fail.

With over 40,000 species currently threatened, and this number rapidly increaseign every year, conservation efforts are sorely needed.

Please take a look at the information on this website and fill in the form to the right for more information on this dire issue.

It is time to act. Conservation efforts are critical to preserving these species.
